The ceremony and reception will be held at Kelly's family home. Located in Walnut Creek CA, it has a beautiful, unobstructed view of the Bay Area.

Parking

There is some room along the south side of Rosemont Court where Rosemont meets Hilltop Crescent. Look for the shuttle that will bring you up the hill and to the house, or make the short walk around Hilltop Crescent and up the hill. Parking will be limited so if you're able, please make arrangements to carpool or Uber.
