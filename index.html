Our Story

The Fourth of July

On the 4th of July 2020 we were lucky enough to get to visit Yosemite National Park, one of our all time favorite places to be. It's an incredibly beautiful place and one that's near and dear to us. At the time, Covid restrictions greatly limited the number of people allowed to visit the park at one time which meant that practically had the whole place to ourselves, it was surreal. So, it seemed like the perfect place to get engaged.
