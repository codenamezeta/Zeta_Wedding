Cocktail Hour

There will be cold beer on tap. Please help yourselves. The beer was home-brewed with extra love by the best man himself. There are two to choose from. The first is an Irish red style, on the malty side but well balanced. The second is an American Pale Ale, hoppy and robust. There is also a selection of red and white wines, including Pinot Grigio, Chardonnay, Merlot, and Cabernet.
